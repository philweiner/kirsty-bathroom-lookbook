Wall B Design Change

The non-shower section of Wall B (behind the toilet) now has Dormido wood effect tiles from floor to 1200 mm, with a light desert-style paint from 1200 mm to ceiling. The fluted green tiles have been removed from this wall. This creates a warm, cohesive look with the shower tiles while keeping the ceiling area light and airy.
